★ DESIGN MANAGEMENT Design management, through a dedicated design manager working with the project manager on behalf of the owner, primarily seeks to establish project management practices that are focused on enhancing the design process across the entire design team. The successful implementation of owner focused design management throughout the entire project lifecycle can represent the difference between the success and failure of the project. ★ TENDERING & PROCUREMENT An effective procurement management and tender process provides a positive evaluation approach that leads not only to the appointment of appropriate consultant, contractor and suppliers but to ensure that the ongoing relationship is mutually beneficial. A wholly balanced and highly efficient tender management process improves the quality of the project while reducing costs and managing risks. ★ CONTRACT MANAGEMENT Since the poor administration of construction contract agreement could jeopardize the overall success of the project, we manage and administer contracts with the highest level of professionalism and with the help of our high-caliber contract expertise. ★ CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT ECB’s treats the design and construction phases as integrated stages; therefore, it is imperative that our CM professionals are involved in the project since the earliest stages of design. The involvement of the CM in the early phases of the project’s design has proven to be efficient and effective, Our CM personnel can offer valuable advice during the design stages that will help avoid any costly change order after the construction contract is awarded. ★ TIME MANAGEMENT We believe that a well-planned job is a half-done job. Therefore, our scheduling and planning professional work on applying a set of strategies and planning techniques to ensure timely completion of all project’s phases and mile stones. Our planning & scheduling consultants understand our client’s ideas and objectives and transform them to program and mile stones. Then they control the program throughout the project’s execution by ensuring that the contractor is in full coherence with the project’s predefined time schedule and plan. ★ COST MANAGEMENT Cost management is one of the most important aspects in a successful project, The main objective of our cost management division is to maintain and control project’s cost within the prescribed projects’ budget. ★ CLAIMS MANAGEMENT Claims and disputes are inevitable in the construction industry. Our job is to early identify those claims and try to advice on any necessary avoidance plan. Our claims management & resolution professional will represent the owner in any claims or disputes that might arise during the project.
